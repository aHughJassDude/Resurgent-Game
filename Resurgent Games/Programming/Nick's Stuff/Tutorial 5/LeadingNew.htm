America, America — The Leading Characters

Nathaniel
played by Max Wilton

Linda
played by Kathy Meade

The two leading characters in America, America are Nathaniel, portrayed by Max Wilton, and Linda, portrayed by Kathy Meade. Both Max Wilton and Kathy Meade starred in the original London cast. Max Wilton was with the production from the start, while Kathy joined the London cast shortly after the play opened to rave reviews more than a year ago. Max also starred in the off-Broadway productions of "The Castle" and "What are You Doing Here?" Kathy Meade began her career as a child actress and model. Her first big role was in the children's spectacular of "Who's Coming for Christmas?" Kathy later starred on TV, playing the role of Debbie on "All My Years, All My Tears."
