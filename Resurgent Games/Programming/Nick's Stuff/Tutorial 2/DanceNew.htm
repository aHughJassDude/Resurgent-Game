MamboSamba Has Many Types of Dance Instruction

At MamboSamba, you can learn all the latest steps in Tango, Country and Western line dancing, freestyle dancing, hip hop, Gaelic Irish Dancing, Latin, Salsa, Swing, Tap and many others. If you are serious about learning ballroom dancing, our instructors are experienced professionals who will help you strive to reach your goals. MamboSamba offers dance instruction at any age. We have our Twinkletoes dance classes for children as young as prekindergarten. We offer training to teens, adults, and have a special discounted session for our Seniors Swing fest.

Learning to Dance is a Great Way to Keep Fit

You can increase your mobility, gain self-confidence, and increase your self-esteem. Want to lose weight? Who doesn't? There is no better way to keep those pounds off by staying in shape by dancing. It's a nonrepetitive, no impact means of staying fit that you can continue practicing for your entire life.
