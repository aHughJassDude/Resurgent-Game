About the Game

Link was just a farm boy in a village called Ordon. One day Zant, the evil and corrupt king of the Twilight Realm, and his minions appeared and attacked the village. They then brought Link into the Twilight Realm where he transformed into a wolf because the realm is magical. He then meets Midna who helps him turn back into himself and helps him on his quest to defeat Zant and save Hyrule.

In his quest, Link travels far off to snowy mountains, a scorching desert, the bottom of a lake, and inside a volcano. He obtains many items to help him on his quest including:

  • a bow and arrows
  • a ball and chain
  • and the legendary Master Sword
